COLLAGE HISTORY

Fulia Govt. ITI is one of the oldest ITI in West Bengal situated at Fulia, Nadia District in India. It was established in 1953, formerly it was known as Fulia polytechnic and in 2003 this institute has been converted as Fulia Govt. ITI, located at 0.5 km from Fulia Railway Station and 0.7 km from NH-34 high way towards North-East side. This Institute is spread over 5 acres of land in a pleasant green environment and offers five NCVT affiliated training courses i.e. i) Fitter, ii) Electrician, iii) Welder, iv) Surveyor and v) Refrigeration and Air-conditioning Technician under the Directorate of Industrial Training, Technical Education, Training and Skill Development Department, Govt. of West Bengal. This Institute has well equipped workshop, IT Lab, Smart Class room and good library etc. This ITI provides high quality training. The placement Cell of this ITI supports students in making the right career choices and enables employers in taking the right hiring decisions during recruitment. Huge number of trainees of this Institute employed in Railway, Ordinance ,ship builders, SAIL and PDCL ,WBSEDCL, WBTDCL .and various state Govt. jobs.
